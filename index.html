Central Library

We take pride in our three-storey, fully computerized library located at the heart of the campus. It offers a wide range of facilities including open stack areas, individual study tables, a dedicated reference section, an e-resources section, a periodicals section, and a photocopying facility. A Book Bank service is also available for intake-wise toppers, socially disadvantaged students, and those in financial need.

The library’s rich collection includes 69,694 textbooks, 4,811 reference books, 3,712 bound volumes, 3,987 general books, more than 25,000 e-books, and over 5,000 e-journals.

Our library staff actively organizes various programs such as Library Workshops, Orientation Programs, Book Exhibitions, Essay Writing Competitions, NDLI User Awareness Programs, IEEE Training Sessions, KNIMBUS Workshops, Wachan Prerana Din, National Reading Day, and the Best User Award. These initiatives encourage reading habits among students and contribute to their academic and professional growth.

Library Rules and Regulations

  1. Library card is compulsory; no transaction will be made without Library card.
  2. Students should swap their Library card on biometric at entrance gate.
  3. Students keep their personal belongings / Bags etc at the property counter.
  4. Personal books are not allowed inside the college Library.
  5. Mobile Phone to be switched off or keep in silent mode while using Library facility.
  6. If the student loses the card, a new card will be issued as per library policy on application from student.
  7. Six books will be issued to the students at a time for a period of 15 days.
  8. If the books are not returned before the stipulated time limit, a penalty will be imposed per day per book as per library policy.
  9. If a book is lost, either it shall be replaced by a new copy or by paying double the cost of the books.
  10. Library materials must be handled with care, as a responsible member.
  11. Students should check the condition of the books before issue, students may be responsible for any damage.
  12. Students should clear their fine before the End Sem exam.
  13. If the requisitioned book is not available, kindly mentioned in the demand register, it will be issued as soon as it is available based on the serial order of claimants.
  14. Important notices and announcements are displayed on notice board.
  15. The library is a place for individual study. Readers should co-operate and keep the library a quite and peaceful place. Silence should be maintained all the time.
  16. Smoking, chewing, spitting, enjoying lunch box in the library is strictly prohibited.
  17. The Librarian may temporarily suspend or permanently withdraw membership, if a member is found disobeying the rules and regulations of the library.
  18. Clearance certificate will be issued only after clearing of all the dues on verifying records.
  19. No photograph of the library shall be taken without the prior permission of the librarian.
  20. The library reserves the right to call back any issued book / item at any time.
  21. In case of any difficulty readers can approach the librarian.
